About Us

your journey We are one of the fastest growing constructions/property developers with over a decades of expertise in building positive experiences for all our stakeholders and winning customer’s trust. Instituted in 2008, we have developed many landmark buildings across both Telugu states Telangana & Andhra :- Hyderabad, Nellore, Kadapa, Bhongir and Yadagiri Gutta for variety of residential projects.

Swetcha constructions includes villas, houses, premium residences, luxury apartments, value homes, independent living for seniors and mixed-use lifestyle enclaves and townships. Swetcha is among the few developers who also enjoys a reputation of developing Grade A quality properties. Swetcha assures you best-in-class design and top-of-the-line quality that exude elegance and sophistication.
